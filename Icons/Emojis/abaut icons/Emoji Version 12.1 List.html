Emoji Version 12.1

Emoji 12.1 was an update to Emoji 12.0 which brought forward a number of gender neutral and hand-holding sequences originally drafted for Emoji 13.0 in 2020.

Emoji 12.1 was released on 2019-10-29, with the relevant data files also published on this date.
